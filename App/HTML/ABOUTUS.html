We are Bred,

We are a small team of university students that made it our goal to create a simpler way for younger people, and the less experienced to manage their finance. Thousands of people across the nation are entering new stages of life where they must learn how to manage their spending on their lonesome. Through the Bred app, people are able to develop healthy spending habits, save money, and overall become more financially independent through the use of payment tracking, budget allocation and graphs/charts which summarise their week/month..

Create an account for free, and join us today to take back the reigns of your own money!
